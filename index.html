Shot and Shot Conversion Rate
2019 FIFA WOmen's World Cup

In this case study, I used data from the 2019 Women's World Cup to evaluate the "average shot percentage per match" and the "average shot conversion rate per match" for the top-ranking teams of the tournament. I discussed the insights and limitations of this data and concluded with suggestions for other analyses that would be beneficial for understanding shooting trends in women's soccer.
